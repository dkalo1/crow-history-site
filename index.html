Evolution & Origins

The crow family (Corvidae) has a rich evolutionary history spanning millions of years, developing into one of the most successful bird families on Earth.

25 Million Years Ago

Early corvid ancestors emerge during the Miocene epoch, branching off from other songbird lineages.

17 Million Years Ago

First true corvids appear in the fossil record, showing primitive crow-like characteristics.

10 Million Years Ago

Major radiation of corvid species begins, spreading across different continents and ecosystems.

2 Million Years Ago

Modern crow species emerge, developing the advanced cognitive abilities we see today.
